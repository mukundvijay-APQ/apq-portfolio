FMEA: PPG Sensor Assembly for a Next-Gen Wearable Ring

Mukund Vijay · Fictional scenario

Situation

During NPI for a next-generation health-monitoring ring, the engineering team designed an 18-path photoplethysmography (PPG) sensor array — multi-color LEDs paired with 3 photodiodes under epoxy encapsulation. This was the most complex optical assembly in the product line. Quality engineering was tasked with conducting a design FMEA before DVT build.

FMEA Analysis

FMEA Scope & Team

Cross-functional team: Quality Engineering (facilitator), Optical Engineering, Mechanical Design, Manufacturing Engineering, Reliability, and CM process engineering. Scope: PPG sub-assembly from LED/photodiode die attach through final encapsulation and optical validation.

LED ALIGNMENT MITIGATION (RPN 160→32)

Design change: Added fiducial marks on substrate for vision-guided pick-and-place — reduces variance from ±75μm to ±25μm Process control: 100% post-placement AOI with ±30μm acceptance criteria SPC: Real-time Cpk monitoring at CM — alert at Cpk < 1.33

LED WAVELENGTH MITIGATION (RPN 162→36)

Incoming inspection: Spectrophotometer verification for every LED reel — acceptance: peak wavelength ±2nm Supplier spec: Tightened LED binning from 5nm to 3nm bin width Firmware: Added wavelength calibration routine compensating for LED-to-LED variation

Control Plan Integration

  • Station 3 (die attach): 100% AOI — auto-reject + recalibration if 3 consecutive OOS
  • Station 5 (encapsulation): Dispense volume SPC + X-ray sampling per AQL 0.15
  • Station 7 (functional test): 100% optical performance — SNR, wavelength response, dark current
  • Incoming: LED wavelength verification per reel; photodiode dark current per lot

Key Takeaways

The FMEA was conducted 8 weeks before DVT build, allowing design changes to be incorporated into production tooling rather than retrofitted. Total cost of FMEA-driven changes: ~$45K. Estimated cost of discovering these issues in PVT or field: $500K+. The discipline of translating every high-RPN item into a specific control plan entry ensured FMEA insights survived the transition to production.
