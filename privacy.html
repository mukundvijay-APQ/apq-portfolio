Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 24, 2026

APQ — AI Powered Quality ("we", "our", or "us") operates the website at mukundvijay-apq.github.io/apq-portfolio. This page describes our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our site.

Information We Collect

When you use the comments feature on our articles, you may sign in using Google or LinkedIn authentication. Through this process, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from the authentication provider. We do not collect passwords or access tokens beyond the authentication session.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected solely to display your name and profile photo alongside your comments, enable comment features such as replies and likes, and identify you as the author of your comments for editing and deletion purposes.

Data Storage

Comment data is stored in Google Firebase Realtime Database. Your public display name and profile photo are visible to other visitors. Your email address is stored but not publicly displayed, and is used only for administrative purposes.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

Google Firebase — for authentication and comment storage. Google's privacy policy applies to their handling of authentication data. LinkedIn — for optional authentication. LinkedIn's privacy policy applies to their handling of authentication data. GitHub Pages — for website hosting.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies necessary for authentication sessions. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

Data Retention & Deletion

Your comment data is retained as long as the comments remain on the site. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us at the email below. As admin, you can also delete your own comments directly through the interface.

Children's Privacy

Our site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy
